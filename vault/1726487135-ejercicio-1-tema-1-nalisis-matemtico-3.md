# Obtén la forma implícita de la vurca cuya paremtrización es $\alpha(t) = (t^2-4,4t)$

$$
\begin{split}
    & \alpha(t) = (t^2-4,4t) = (x(t), y(t)) \\
    & x = t^2 - 4 \to  x = \left( \frac{y}{4} \right)^2 -4 \implies x= \frac{y^2}{16} - 4\\
    & y = 4t \to t = \frac{y}{4}\\
    & \implies 16x - y^2 + 64 = 0
\end{split}
$$
