# Determina la expresión implícita de la curva parametrizada por $\alpha (t) = (\sec (t), \tan (t))$

$$
\begin{split}
    & \alpha(t) = (\sec (t), \tan (t))\\
    & x = \sec(t) \implies x^2 = \sec ^2(t) = \frac{1}{\cos ^2(t)} = \tan ^2(t) + 1 = y^2 + 1\\
    & y = \tan(t)\\
    & \implies x^2-y^2 - 1 = 0
\end{split}
$$
